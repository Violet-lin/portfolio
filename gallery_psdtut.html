Adobe Tutorial Microsite
Sitting in the development team within Sky UK Ltd, I realised that many of my teammates were not familiar with handling complex Adobe Photoshop / Illustrator files. Time was wasted waiting for the design team to extract accurate assets that are ready for development. Some developers spent hours fiddling around the complicated image layers rather than focusing on coding. I have decided to create a microsite to include quick tips and tricks for some of the frequent-occurring problems.
This microsite included:
• Use-case specific tutorials for developers
• Step by step photoshop UI walkthrough
• Collection of Photoshop automation
• Mobile-compatible for easy reference

This portal has been continuously updated based on the team’s feedback to further streamline the creative production process.
