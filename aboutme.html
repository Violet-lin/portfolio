About Me

Hi, I'm Violet. My hobbies are drawings, sculpting, kayaking and reading. I have an increased interest in repairing and troubleshooting ever since I worked in the UK. "Never give up", "There's always a way to improve" are mottos that I live by. Working many years in both agency and in-house environment, I feel that collaboration is the key to successful team and projects.
