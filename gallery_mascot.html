Mascot Design for Social Campaign
Conceptualise and developed visual assets for educational social media campaigns by Coopervision. My design process involved creating a campaign mascot, defining the storyboard for the comic, and create the final Facebook post content, at all stages both external and internal stakeholders were involved to ensure a smooth flow from initial brainstorming to final content delivery. The mascot was used over 8 weeks in Facebook content to educate and engage fans on eyecare trivial, particularly popular in East Asia e.g Taiwan Hong Kong.
