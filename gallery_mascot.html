Mascot Design for Social Campaign
Conceptualise and developed visual assets for educational social media campaign by Coopervision. My design process involved creating a campaign mascot, defining the storyboard for the comic, and create the final facebook post content, at all stages both external and internal stackholders were involved to ensure a smooth flow from initial brainstorming to final content delivery. The mascot was use over 8 weeks in facebook book content o educate and enagage fans on eyecare trival, particularly popular in East Asia e.g Taiwan Hong Kong.
