Immersive user interface design revamp
After creating a browser-based RPG game - STAR BLAZERS -, we were asked to revamp the internal user portal to match the look and feel of the game. I provided guidance on how we can best replicate the design aesthetic of the game in the new user interface, ensuring that the boys in the Boy Bridgage feel like they are operating the control panel of the ship from the STAR BLAZERS game.
